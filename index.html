Welcome to SKKU Visualization Laboratory!

We design, develop, and evaluate interactive visualization systems that bridge humans and computers. The ever-increasing size and complexity of data are now challenging our perceptual and cognitive abilities in daily analytics, leaving the data and models intractable without support. We believe interactive visualization systems can address these challenges by amplifying our abilities to understand such data and models. We research Information Visualization (InfoVis), Visual Analytics, and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI).

We are looking for undergraduate interns and graduate students. If you are interested in research topics such as scalable and responsive visualization systems, explanatory data visualization, and human-in-the-loop machine learning, please contact Prof. Jaemin Jo (jmjo@skku.edu).
